Tasks Legend

Event
An event occuring at a specific date. Meant to structure time rather than to define work.
Task
An item of work. Subject to migration rules.
Completed Task
A successfully finished task. A deleted task should be struck-through.
Scheduled Task
A task with planned future work. When the schedule is confirmed, mark the event as a task on the appropriate parent planner on the right date.
Migrated Task
A task that has successfully gone through the migration plan below.
Signifiers
Used to add information to a task. From right to left...
priority: mark a task as important
inspiration: an interesting thought or idea
explore: requires further research
Migration
Migrate tasks as months pass to keep information up-to-date. Start by looking through previous monthly planners- mark tasks as complete or strike out those that won't get completed. Migrate incomplete tasks to the next month. Then, check the future planner for tasks marked for the upcoming month. Move them to the next month, too.
